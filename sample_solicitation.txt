SOLICITATION NUMBER: W912QR-24-R-0001
TITLE: Comprehensive Cybersecurity Support Services
AGENCY: Department of Defense - Defense Information Systems Agency
NAICS CODE: 541512 - Computer Systems Design Services
SET-ASIDE: Small Business, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB)
SECURITY CLEARANCE: Secret Required

DESCRIPTION:
The Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) requires comprehensive cybersecurity support services to protect critical information systems and infrastructure. The contractor shall provide expert-level cybersecurity services including threat detection, incident response, security operations center (SOC) support, and continuous monitoring capabilities.

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) implementation and management
- Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response (SOAR) platform operations
- 24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC) monitoring and analysis
- Incident response and forensic analysis capabilities
- Zero Trust Architecture implementation support
- Cloud security for AWS, Azure, and hybrid environments
- Vulnerability assessment and penetration testing
- Security compliance and audit support (NIST, FedRAMP, CMMC)
- Threat intelligence analysis and integration
- Network security monitoring and intrusion detection

REQUIRED CAPABILITIES:
- Proven experience with MITRE ATT&CK framework
- Expertise in SIEM platforms (Splunk, QRadar, or equivalent)
- SOAR platform experience (Phantom, Demisto, or equivalent)
- Cloud security expertise (AWS, Azure, GCP)
- DoD cybersecurity experience required
- Active Secret clearance for all personnel
- CISSP, CEH, or equivalent certifications
- DevSecOps pipeline integration experience

PAST PERFORMANCE:
Offerors must demonstrate successful completion of at least three (3) similar cybersecurity projects within the past five (5) years for federal government agencies. Projects should include SOC operations, incident response, and security monitoring.

CONTRACT TYPE: Firm Fixed Price
PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE: Base year plus four (4) option years
ESTIMATED VALUE: $5,000,000 - $10,000,000
PLACE OF PERFORMANCE: Fort Meade, MD with remote work options

RESPONSE DEADLINE: 60 days from posting date

KEY EVALUATION FACTORS:
1. Technical Approach and Understanding (40%)
2. Past Performance and Relevant Experience (30%)
3. Key Personnel Qualifications (20%)
4. Price (10%)

SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS:
- All personnel must maintain active Secret clearance
- Contractor must have existing FSO and facility clearance
- Must comply with CMMC Level 3 requirements
- System must achieve Authority to Operate (ATO)

This is a competitive procurement reserved for Small Business and SDVOSB concerns. Interested offerors should review the complete solicitation package on SAM.gov and submit proposals through the designated portal.
